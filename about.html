Our Story

Built by women,
for women.

We started Sync'd because we were tired of apps that treated our cycles as problems to solve rather than rhythms to understand.

"Every woman deserves to understand her own body."

Our Mission

Making cycle awareness accessible to all.

Sync'd was born from a simple observation: most women don't understand their menstrual cycles beyond the basics. We're changing that through gentle education, not medical jargon.

Our approach is rooted in wellness, not diagnosis. We believe that understanding your hormonal patterns can transform how you eat, train, work, and rest‚Äîwithout creating anxiety or fear.
